Internship and Job Search Planning

Quick Tips for the Job Search

  • Set Weekly Goals:

    Block out time on your calendar for job search tasks—small, consistent effort beats last-minute sprints.

  • Customize Every Application:

    Tailor your resume and cover letter for each position. Use keywords from the job posting for maximum impact!

  • Track Your Applications:

    Update your profile, connect with alumni, and engage with organizations you admire. Pro tip: let recruiters know you’re open to opportunities.

  • Practice Your Pitch:

    Prepare a short introduction about yourself for networking events and interviews—it’s your highlight reel!

  • Stay Positive and Persistent:

    Rejection is part of the process—don’t let it slow you down. Seek support and keep moving forward!
