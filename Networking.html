Networking & Building Connections

Expand Your Professional Horizons

Networking isn’t just about trading business cards or connecting on LinkedIn—it’s about building meaningful relationships. At UMSI, your network can help you discover hidden opportunities, learn directly from experienced professionals, and form connections that last well beyond graduation.

Whether you’re an introvert or a natural conversationalist, the CDO provides tools, events, and strategies to make networking approachable and rewarding. From informal coffee chats with alumni to industry speaker sessions and career fairs, there’s a way for every student to start building their professional community.

Ways to Build Your Network

  1. Attend UMSI and University Events: Workshops, employer info sessions, and career fairs are designed for you to meet professionals and employers in a friendly, low-pressure setting.
  2. Tap into Alumni Networks: UMSI graduates love helping current students. Use Michigan Mentorship Network or Handshake to seek advice or set up informational interviews.
  3. Join Student & Professional Organizations: Clubs on Maize Pages and local chapters of information-related associations connect you with like-minded peers and industry experts.
  4. Connect on LinkedIn: Reach out to classmates, alumni, and employers. Follow companies and organizations to stay up to date on opportunities and trends.
  5. Schedule Coffee Chats: Short conversations (virtual or in person) are a great way to learn about someone’s career journey and expand your network organically.
